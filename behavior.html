Behavior & Training

Scratching

Provide sturdy scratching posts and reward their use. Place near favorite sleeping spots.

Play Aggression

Use wand toys for distance; stop sessions before overstimulation.

Litter Issues

Rule out medical causes first; keep boxes clean; try different box types and litter textures.
